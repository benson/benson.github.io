          Field manual // 01

          HOW TO SURVIVE

          01

          Move, aim, cast

          Move with WASD or arrow keys. Your signature weapon follows the cursor; C lets that weapon track a nearby target instead. Other weapons keep their own seeking, orbit, lane, or area behavior. E and R are active abilities.

          02

          Build the machine

          Collect cyan data motes to level up. Pick weapons and passive stats. A level-five weapon plus its matching passive can evolve when an elite drops an access key.

          03

          Hold for the apex

          Enemy tiers and wave density rise throughout the operation. Survive the timer, then defeat the map's apex before its five-minute enrage.

          04

          Stay active when downed

          In co-op, crawl toward safety, press E to shield a nearby standing ally, and use G to call for help. A squadmate inside your ring completes the rescue in three seconds.
