Field manual // 01

HOW TO SURVIVE

01

Move, aim, cast

Move with WASD or arrow keys. Your signature weapon fires automatically toward the cursor. Tap C to toggle auto-aim. E and R are active abilities.

02

Build the machine

Collect cyan data motes to level up. Pick weapons and passive stats. A level-five weapon plus its matching passive can evolve when an elite drops an access key.

03

Hold for the apex

Enemy tiers and wave density rise throughout the operation. Survive the timer, then defeat the map's apex before its five-minute enrage.

04

Revive the squad

In co-op, stand inside a downed ally's ring for ten seconds to revive them. If every specialist is down at once, the operation is lost.
