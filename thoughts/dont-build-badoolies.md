# don't build badoolies
yeah, you COULD just build and build and build without ever thinking about who you're building for. but i'm going to take that time saved and just measure twice so that when i cut it actually matters

"check out this cool demo" and the demo is a badoolie. (my grandpa would describe the thingamajigs i would build out of wooden k'nex as "badoolies", potentially from the french "bidule", at least according to Claude). this is what people build. it's a thing that spins around and moves parts and connects some API to some other API... but not because someone needs that, but just because it's fun to stick two pieces together and spin them around until one of them falls off.

it's so so easy now to build these badoolies. and it's fun! it's always been fun to take two blocks and stack them on top of each other. but recognize it for what it is - play - and realize that's not usually the right way to build something that other people want to pay you for.
